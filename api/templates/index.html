Brand

Hemay

Hemay is an international animal medical product, Sino-Singapore cooperative product enterprise. The core competitiveness focuses on the design and production of animal orthopedic equipment, and actively integrates the latest foreign technology and innovative products and solutions for extensive cooperation with domestic and foreign medical experts.

Research

The purpose of research is to improve animal welfare and provide animals with a higher quality of life. After decades of design and production experience, the company has become China's leading manufacturer of Department of orthopedics equipment. At present, our company brand provides medical institutions with a full range of special steel plates and screws and various special animal patent products, which are suitable for bone repair of small and medium-sized animals in different parts. The invention of various orthopedic instruments makes doctors' operations more scientific convenient, fast, and making the postoperative effect more ideal.

Products

Our products cover mainland China, Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, Southeast Asian countries, and have entered Australia, Europe, North and South America and Africa. The classic brand of instruments and equipment products are used in teaching and treatment of major animal science research institutes, provincial and municipal animal husbandry stations, and police canine bases in China.

Products

Our product range includes Department of orthopedics instruments, bone plate screws, bone marrow needle, steel wire, disposal platform, common instrument box, special instrument box, etc.
